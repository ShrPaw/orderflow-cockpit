# Screenshot Guide

## Browser Setup

- **Browser:** Chrome or Firefox (latest)
- **Window size:** 1920×1080 or 1440×900
- **Zoom level:** 100%
- **DevTools:** Closed
- **Theme:** Dark (default)

## Screenshots to Capture

### 1. Full Dashboard — Live BTCUSDT

**What:** The complete app with all panels visible — chart, heatmap, side panel, Time & Sales.

**State:** BTCUSDT live, all connection dots green, LIVE pill visible.

**Browser:** Maximized window.

**Caption:** "Orderflow Cockpit — live BTCUSDT orderflow visualization"

---

### 2. Zoomed-In Candle Inspection

**What:** Chart zoomed in to show 5-10 visible candles with footprint cells and wick detail.

**State:** Scroll wheel to zoom in until footprint text labels appear. Pan to a busy area.

**Caption:** "Deep zoom — footprint cells and wick detail at candle level"

---

### 3. Bubble Tooltip Visible

**What:** Mouse hovering over a large trade bubble with the tooltip showing.

**State:** Wait for a large trade (check Time & Sales for whale trades), hover over the corresponding bubble.

**Caption:** "Bubble tooltip — aggressive flow event inspection"

---

### 4. Heatmap with Liquidity Bands

**What:** Heatmap panel showing bid/ask bands with quantity labels.

**State:** Healthy order book (green dots), heatmap not stale.

**Caption:** "Liquidity depth heatmap with quantity labels and spread indicator"

---

### 5. GO LIVE Detached State

**What:** Chart panned away from live edge with amber GO LIVE pill visible.

**State:** Drag chart to the left until GO LIVE pill appears in top-right corner.

**Caption:** "GO LIVE indicator — return to live edge from historical inspection"

---

### 6. Connection Health

**What:** Toolbar showing connection dots and status.

**State:** All dots green, LIVE pill on chart. Optionally show the connection status bar during initial connect.

**Caption:** "Per-stream connection health indicators"

---

### 7. Symbol Selector

**What:** Asset selector modal open with instrument list.

**State:** Click the symbol name in toolbar to open the selector.

**Caption:** "Symbol selector — 24+ Binance Futures perpetuals"

---

## What NOT to Capture

- Browser DevTools or console (unless documenting a bug)
- Personal bookmarks or extensions
- Other tabs or windows
- Network/IP information
- Any trading bot or signal overlay

## Suggested Captions

Keep captions factual and technical. Do not claim:
- Trading profits
- Predictive accuracy
- Signal quality
- "AI-powered" anything

Good captions describe what the tool does, not what it claims to achieve.
