# Orderflow Cockpit — Brand Rationale

## Selected Concept: Liquidity Field

---

### The Idea

Parallel flow lines bending around an invisible center of gravity — like magnetic field lines or fluid dynamics around a pressure point. The lines converge and intensify near the center, suggesting density, depth, and invisible market forces shaping flow. Scattered density nodes sit along the field lines, representing detected liquidity concentrations.

### Why It's Original

Nobody in fintech uses field-line physics as a logo concept. It's abstract, scientific, and memorable. It suggests the product visualizes forces that exist but cannot be seen with the naked eye. It's not a chart, not a dashboard, not a radar — it's a field. You don't read it; you feel it.

### Why It Fits Orderflow Cockpit

Order flow is fundamentally about pressure, density, and imbalance. Field lines naturally represent these concepts:
- **Where lines converge** → pressure is highest, liquidity is dense
- **Where lines diverge** → liquidity is thin, gaps exist
- **The central gravity point** → the invisible force shaping all flow
- **Density nodes** → detected liquidity concentrations in the field

The product reveals what is normally hidden beneath price. The Liquidity Field mark is that idea made visible: invisible forces rendered as structure.

### Why It's Better Than a Generic Chart/Cockpit Logo

A cockpit logo = airplane dashboard = boring, expected.
A candlestick logo = retail trading = low credibility.
A chart icon = every other trading app = invisible.

The Liquidity Field mark says: "This tool shows you the forces that shape price, not the price itself." It positions the product as a field-level intelligence system — something that sees the physics of the market, not just its surface.

### In a Dark Dashboard UI

The layered field lines integrate naturally into dark trading interfaces. The cold teal on near-black feels native to the environment — it doesn't fight with heatmaps, order books, or flow visualizations. It belongs there, like a watermark of the product's core concept.

### On Upwork

This mark communicates scientific sophistication. It signals "professional data visualization" and "market microstructure" — not "crypto signal group" or "retail trading bot." Clients looking for serious order flow tools will immediately recognize the visual language of physics and precision.

## Typography

**Primary:** Inter / Helvetica Neue / Geist

| Element | Weight | Size | Letter-spacing |
|---------|--------|------|----------------|
| ORDERFLOW | 300 (Light) | 42px | 10px |
| COCKPIT | 200 (ExtraLight) | 26px | 16px |

**Fallback Stack:**
```
font-family: 'Inter', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Helvetica', 'Arial', sans-serif;
```

**Rationale:** Geometric, clean, slightly technical without being monospace or sci-fi. The light weight and generous letter-spacing create a premium, airy feel. The weight contrast between ORDERFLOW (300) and COCKPIT (200) creates hierarchy without size difference feeling forced.
